Welcome to Nerd Hangman!

Here are the rules

  1. You will be given a word. Enter a letter that you think is in that word.
  2. If it's right, it will show up on the screen in the center. If not, it will show up to the side.
  3. If you don't solve the word in 12 guesses, then you lose a life
  4. Lose 3 lives, then you lose the game.
